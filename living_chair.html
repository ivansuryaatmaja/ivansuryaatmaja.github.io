Designed in 1940 by the collaboration of Charles E and Eero Saarinen, the sculptural Organic chair was far ahead of its times

While it helped to develop their ideas on moulded plywood and organically shaped shells for their later work, manufacturing constraints meant that the Organic chair couldn't be mass products at the time

It was only in 2006 that the Organic chair was finally put into production

The sculpted back and winglet shaped armrests make it an exceptionally comfortable reading chair

It can also be used as an accent chair for the living room or bedroom
